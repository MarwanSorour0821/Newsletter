    "The difference between wealth and poverty is financial literacy"

    Fiduciary: someone who manages money or property for someone else

    Balance Sheet: to a financial statement that reports a company's assets, liabilities, and shareholder equity at a specific point in time

    Bond: is a fixed-income instrument and investment product where individuals lend money to a government or company at a certain interest rate for an amount of time

    Acquisition: is a transaction wherein one company purchases most or all of another company's shares to gain control of that company
